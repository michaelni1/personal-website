My interests & passions

Interests


  • Technology: Literally everything about it. It's incredible that people in cities and towns in every country on Earth are connected by the same websites, electronics, and operating systems. The characters "www." form such an iconic part of the human experience, and I'm grateful to be able to study something so transformative and powerful.

  • The social sciences: Anything psychology, sociology, law, political science, cognitive science, etc. How do we live together as we get closer together? In what ways is a mind like a computer and what are the implications? What are our heuristics and biases? So many questions, so little time. And what's exciting is that technology continues to frame the way we understand and answer these social issues.

  • Other interests/hobbies: Running, cooking, watching movies, and building programs and websites -- all amazing and wonderful!
